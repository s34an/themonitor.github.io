By: Flores, Kryz Dhaniel P.


Bringing together a diverse group of participants to address key issues related to student achievement and school improvement, Muntinlupa Science High School (MSHS) hostec a comprehensive stakeholders meeting on February 15, 2025 at school gymnasium.

Attendees also included school administrators, faculty members, non-teaching personnel, and parents. The meeting, structured around a formal program, aimed to foster collaboration and shared responsibility for the ongoing success of MunSci.
